ਇੰਸਟਾਲੇਸ਼ਨ -- ACPI ਆਯੋਗ

Current hardware usually requires ACPI (Advanced Configuration and Power Interface) to control the interrupt handling. ACPI completely replaces the old APM system.

Select ਇੰਸਟਾਲੇਸ਼ਨ -- ACPI ਆਯੋਗ if you encounter problems during boot of the kernel. Known problems with machines that have problems with ACPI are:

  • kernel freezes when booting
  • PCI Cards are not detected or initialized properly

You may also try the ਫਾਇਰਮਵੇਅਰ ਟੈਸਟ boot option and look at the test results of the ACPI validation.
